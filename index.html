Products

The Echo of Realms set is a sensory installation that uses atmospheric scents to recreate the environments of beloved stories within your physical space. Housed in sleek, matte-black glass diffusers with charcoal reeds, the collection features sophisticated blends like Old Library & Rain and Midnight Forest, designed to complement a minimalist home aesthetic. Each bottle is etched with coordinates to a fictional location, serving as a subtle, artistic portal to another world through the power of fragrance.

The Ink & Ember journal is a premium artifact designed for the modern narrator, featuring a durable leather-bound cover and heavy-weight cream paper that prevents ink bleed. Beyond its minimalist exterior, the journal contains "hidden" narrative prompts and a wax-sealed letter from an original fictional world, transforming a standard writing experience into an immersive creative journey. It is a tactile companion for readers who wish to bridge the gap between their own thoughts and the expansive realms they explore.
